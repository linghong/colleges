Visit of Five Colleges

On October 2012, I visited five colleges located in Amherst area. They are Amherst College, Hampshire College, Mount Holyoke College, Smith College, UMass Amherst. They worked like a same unit, students can take courses in any campus, buses are also running between each colleges.
